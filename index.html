About

About Our Game

We created a 2D combat game where players can work together to fight against enemy ghost pirates while on a boat. Both players are crucial in that they both have their own unique abilities. Player 1 must defeat all of the ghosts since they have fighting abilities, and player 2 has a shield and can block attacks from ghosts.

Why We Built It

Purpose

🤝

Teamwork

Our game is built around the idea that no one wins alone. Player 1 and Player 2 each hold abilities the other lacks — together they're unstoppable, apart they fall.

🎮

Collaboration in Design

Six students across three grade levels worked together to design, build, and ship this game — itself a living example of the "Better Together" theme.

⚔️

Meaningful Mechanics

Every mechanic serves the theme. The shield and the sword are useless without each other — a design choice that reflects the spirit of cooperative play.
