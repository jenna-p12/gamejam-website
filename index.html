Gameplay

How It Works

Anvay put here

01

Fight the Ghost Pirates

Ghost pirates swarm the deck. Player 1 has the fighting ability to take them down. Player 2 carries a shield, blocking incoming hits and healing both players when damage lands. Neither one can do it without the other.

Anvay put here
Anvay put here

02

Find the Key

When a pirate dies, one of them drops a key. The catch: you have no idea which one. Defeat the right pirate and the rest vanish. The key unlocks a portal hidden deep inside the ship's cabin.

03

Face the Skeleton

At the end waits a skeleton with a parrot on its shoulder. It fires fireballs in every direction: up, down, fast. Both players have to jump, dodge, and push through together. Win, and the treasure is yours.

Dev Log

Behind the Game

Ghost Tide was built for the CIS Academy Spring Game Jam 2026 around the theme "Better Together." The whole design comes from that idea that Player 1 can't heal and Player 2 can't fight. You need each other to survive.

Six students across three grade levels built this: two juniors, two sophomores, two freshmen. The storyboard below shows how the game was planned before a single line of code was written.
